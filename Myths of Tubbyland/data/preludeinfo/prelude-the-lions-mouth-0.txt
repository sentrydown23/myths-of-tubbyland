You made it.
Somehow, against all odds, you escaped the mainland, Custards in hand, Tinky Winky's footsteps faded behind you as the old satellite station emerged from the fog. 

Rusted dishes creaked in the wind above, still pointing toward a sky that no longer seemed to answer.
For the first time in hours, you thought you were safe. 
Then you heard movement. Not outside.

Inside.
